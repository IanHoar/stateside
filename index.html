About Me

I'm Melissa Robison, a hairstylist with over 25 years of experience and a deep appreciation for hair that feels natural, effortless, and personal. I specialize in bridal and wedding hair, as well as custom cutting, coloring, balayage, gray blending, root touch-ups, and dimensional highlights.

My work is rooted in thoughtful consultation and refined technique. I consider face shape, lifestyle, maintenance needs, and budget to create hair that wears beautifully, grows out softly, and feels true to the person wearing it.

Outside the salon, I find inspiration in painting, cooking, hiking, and exploring new places. Originally from Canada, I now call the U.S. home, embracing a life shaped by creativity, curiosity, and connection.

I look forward to creating something beautiful together.
